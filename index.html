FAQ
Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to know how to code?
No. Botwave agents are ready to deploy out of the box. Just pick an agent, connect your Telegram (or other platform), and you're live. The Builder and Boss tiers let you customize, but it's optional.
What platforms are supported?
Telegram (primary), Discord, Slack, Signal, iMessage, Microsoft Teams, Matrix, Web Chat, Zalo, and Voice. New platforms are added regularly.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your conversations and business data stay between you and your agent. We don't harvest, sell, or train on your data. The Boss tier offers fully offline local models for zero-cloud operation.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account.
What AI models do the agents use?
Botwave uses a combination of leading open-source models optimized for each agent's specialty. Boss tier customers can bring their own models and run them entirely on their own hardware.
