Pricing FAQ

Can I stay on Free forever?
Yes. Free never expires. 1 agent on Telegram with local model support. Most personal users never need to upgrade.
What does "self-hosted" mean for pricing?
Agents run on YOUR hardware. We don't charge per API call or token. The subscription unlocks features and support. Run local models for $0/month in AI costs.
Is there a free trial for Boss?
14-day Boss trial, full access. No credit card to start. Cancel anytime — no charge.
Do annual plans include a discount?
Annual saves ~17% (2 months free). Builder: $288/yr. Boss: $492/yr. Operator: $996/yr.
What happens if I cancel?
Your agents keep running — they're on your hardware. You lose premium features but scripts, configs, and data stay on your machine. 30-day money-back guarantee.
Boss vs Operator?
Boss = unlimited agents + full platform. Operator adds 4 security tools (BountyHunter, PentestPro, IntelOps, AuditBot), encrypted workspaces, approval gates, compliance. Security work = Operator.
